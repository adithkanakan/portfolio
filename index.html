Projects

Inclusive Education · Multi-Modal Generative AI Research · Under Review
Dysgraphi.AI

An adaptive multi-modal writing system for neurodivergent learners, built as part of a 4-member research team. Designed specifically for individuals with dysgraphia, the platform delivers real-time AI-powered feedback across typing, handwriting, and speech input modes. It combines NLP-driven error detection, Google Cloud Vision OCR, and gamified learning exercises to support inclusive education. Submitted for publication in Springer Nature's Education and Information Technologies journal.
